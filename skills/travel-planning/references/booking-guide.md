# Booking Optimization Guide

## Timing — When to Book

| Booking Type | Optimal Window | Why |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| Domestic flights | 6–8 weeks out | Price sweet spot before demand peaks |
| International flights | 3–4 months out | 10–20% savings vs last-minute |
| Hotels | 2–3 months out | Best selection + rates; free cancellation available |
| Rental cars | 2–6 weeks out | Prices fluctuate less; last-minute often fine |
| Group activities | 4–6 weeks out | Availability for large groups |
| Popular restaurants | 4–8 weeks out | Tasting menus and hot restaurants book out fast |
| Theme parks / timed entry | 1–3 months out | Avoid sold-out dates |

## Cost Optimization Tactics

| Strategy | Typical Savings | When to Use |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| Shoulder season travel | 30–40% on hotels/flights | Flexible on dates |
| Off-airport car rental | 30–40% vs airport pick-up | Any rental; take taxi/transit to off-site lot |
| Kitchen accommodation | 30%+ on food costs | Family trips, 5+ days, self-catering destination |
| City passes | 20–40% on activities | 3+ attractions planned in one city |
| Open-jaw flights | $0–$100 more than round-trip | Multi-city trips with different start/end points |
| Mid-week flight booking | 5–15% vs weekend | Search Tuesdays; avoid Friday/Sunday searches |
| Bundle hotel + flight | 10–20% | OTA package deals; verify cancellation terms |
| Points / miles redemption | 50–90% on premium cabins | Long-haul business/first class sweet spots |

## Group Booking Tips

- **Flights:** Book in groups of 2, not all together. One problem shouldn't cancel everyone.
- **Hotels:** Request adjoining rooms at booking time. Confirm 48h before arrival. Get written confirmation.
- **Activities:** Ask for group discounts at 8–10+ people — most venues have unpublished group rates.
- **Car rentals:** Compare 2 medium vehicles vs 1 large van — often cheaper and more flexible for splitting routes.
- **Restaurants:** For groups of 6+, always call ahead; most require reservations and have set menus for large parties.
